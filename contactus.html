Visitor's Information

The institute is located in IIIT Guwahati, Bongora, Guwahati- 781015, near Guwahati international airport.

Air India, Jet Airways, Air Asia, IndiGo, SpiceJet, GoAir and Vistara operate regular flights to and from Guwahati.
